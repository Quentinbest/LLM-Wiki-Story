## Major Issues (strongly recommended before final)
1. **2.2 (sister) under-delivers its antagonism** — antagonism-tester — the only escalation dip in the book (margin −1). The sister reads as *distracted* (phone, work call) rather than *failing to see him*. Fix: stage one beat where her eyes pass from the TV bounty-face to his face and **still** file him as "stressed brother" — an active near-recognition that closes. Model it on the excellent 2.1 "哥哥→家属" self-erasure. (Scene-level deepening.)
2. **Narrated-realization / explanation-gloss cluster** — cliche-hunter + subtext-whisperer (independent convergence) — the persona's #1 forbidden move ("他知道 / 不是X…是Y / 听懂了…底下的意思"). Behavior is given, then a gloss translates it into plain text. All fixes are **cuts**:
   - **3.8** (worst): cut "她不是在问'你是谁'。她是在问…" segment AND "和早上那杯咸豆浆是同一个动作——他又一次选择不说自己是谁" segment. **Keep** the antithesis line "他对她的整个生活了如指掌。她对他这个人一无所知" (earns its directness, subtitle-cold).
   - **3.8** open: cut the "他要是开口，她会听见一个声音…不想让那个声音也有机会被她再不认出一次" causal explanation; keep "他想说一句'奶奶您慢点'…他没说。"
   - **4.6**: cut "他差一点，对着那扇关上的绿门，说出自己的名字" — let "嘴唇张开了一点" collide directly with "他没有说" (the single highest-value cut).
   - **2.7**: the closing line + scene title "他知道它在哪儿" is the literal forbidden "他知道" construction; externalize the ending (end on "手搁在口袋边沿") and consider retitling.
   - **3.6**: cut "陈默听懂了那句话底下的意思：外面不打，是因为里面更好办" (preserve duel-as-small-talk disguise).
   - **3.4 / 4.2**: trim the bare "身体比脑子快——三年了…" / "这是他这辈子的第一反应：…" gloss; keep the concrete object-list ("工牌、工号、App、身份证").
   - **3.1**: cut "映出他自己的半张脸" (the one screen-reflection-as-divided-self flinch) and "但他知道这一条."
