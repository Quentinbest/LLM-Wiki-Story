# Voice Anchors — 魔尊账号

## Sentence rhythm

- **Short declarative stacks (3–7 characters)** for tension/action beats. One fact per line. No subordinate clauses in climax scenes.
- **Dash as pivot marker (`——`)** for the moment the Gap opens — the character sees what they didn't expect, then acts.
- **Mid-length (10–20 chars) for routine/flat** — delivery descriptions, system notifications, procedural beats.
- **Fragment sentences for system responses** — "短。机械。" — three periods, each a beat in time.
- **Montage density shift**: dense (3 days in 3 sentences) → sparse (7 days in one beat) → expand (final day, full beat). See Scene 4.5.

## Tonal range

- **Primary: deadpan baseline** ("基线"). Neither sad nor triumphant. The story's ironies are structural, not signaled.
- **Tonal ceiling: procedural crispness** — the register of someone reading a delivery confirmation. Flat, professional, accurate.
- **Tonal floor (deepest irony): the almost-unsaid** — Scene 4.4's footstep recognition, 4.6's lips-opening beat. The most important beats are the ones the character *doesn't* complete.
- **No lyrical register** at any point. No metaphor drawn from nature, weather, or emotion. Metaphors only from the technological/mundane layer (TV static, sticker, sticker).
- **Comedy: structural, not verbal**. The humor comes from systems treating identity as a bug report (Scene 4.3), not from jokes.

## Forbidden words/moves

- **NO narrator certifying emotion**: "他苦笑", "他感到", "他遗憾", "他明白了". Any micro-emotion tag ruins the deadpan.
- **NO "没有+verb" for character choice**: Exception only for observable absence of speech/action (a foot that didn't move, a word not spoken when lips parted). Never for "他没去找" / "他没想过".
- **NO cross-scene narrator ranking**: "这是他第一次..." "比之前更..." — no narrator drawing comparisons across scenes.
- **NO lyrical theme-naming**: No philosophical epigrams. No "这就是生活" or "他终于懂了". The Controlling Idea is felt, not said.
- **NO retrospection in Resolution**: Scene 4.5-4.6 must not reference Lin Sheng, Su Han, or sister. No internal callback. The callback is structural (incident mirror, not memory).
- **NO sighs**: "他吸了一口气" flagged as emotional management in final scene — cut in revision.
- **NO romantic natural light**: Indoor = corridor fluorescent (感应灯). No sunset, no sunrise, no cinematic shafts. Douyin blue light permitted (diegetic screen glow).
- **NO music**: No "音乐起" or implied soundtrack. Silence is the audio motif.

## Reference voices

- **裴鸣 (author persona)**: Enclosed-space dialogue duels. Behavior-only narration. No interior monologue. "决斗伪装成闲聊" — the violence of the scene is in what's NOT said. "前面是绕路，然后一刀" — long procedural setup, one sharp turn. Minimum defensible action.
- **Lin Sheng's dialogue register**: Precise, protocol-compliant, never rude. His politeness is the weapon. No emotional leakage. He completes tasks.
- **Su Han's register**: Cynical, quick, diagnostic. Her lines diagnose the world's state; she doesn't offer solutions.
- **System voices**: Platform app, intercom broadcast, Douyin — flat, mechanical, authoritative without hostility. The systems are not evil; they're indifferent. That's worse.
- **Woman at 603**: "放门口就行" — the most important line in the book, spoken as a completely harmless daily sentence. The violence is structural, not textual.

## Hand/body proxy system

- **Hands carry all interior content**: Key (Scene 3.8), door handle (Scene 3.8), phone (4.1-4.6), intercom button (4.3), strap-tightening (4.6), pocket reflex suppression (4.2), Moleskine rubber band (4.4).
- **Micro-gestures over macro**: The left hand drifting toward the pocket and stopping (4.2) carries more weight than any narrated hesitation. The lips parting and closing (4.6) carries the entire Controlling Idea.
- **The body is never described in repose**: No "he leaned against the wall and thought." Either the body is in motion or it's in standby (waiting, listening).

## Light motif system

- **感应灯 (12-second cycle)**: The structural metronome. Used in 4.1, 4.2, 4.3, 4.4. The light turns off at the moment of revelation.
- **Intercom blue light → white**: Scene 4.1 establishes the blue standby light; Scene 4.3 changes it to white when the system connects. The scratch on the panel (4.1) is still visible under the light (4.4).
- **Douyin blue light (4.6)**: Diegetic, not atmospheric. The customer's attention is on her phone, not on the delivery man.
- **Phone screen light (final image after revision)**: Removed the generic "light on face" ending. The strap-tightening gesture is the closing image.

## Key structural echoes

- **"你好，请问你是魔尊·墨渊吗？" (Scene 1.2)** → **"放门口就行" (Scene 4.6)** — from question+face+wrong-name to command+no-face+no-name.
- **奶茶两杯+炸鸡, 602, 18:47 (1.2)** → **麻辣烫+柠檬茶两杯, 603, 17:42 (4.5)** — same building, neighbor door, same quantity, one hour earlier.
- **6 floors up (1.2, toward inciting incident)** → **6 floors down (4.6, toward continuation)** — elevator still broken, no one waiting on 8-9 landing.
- **App name bar 4 appearances**: frozen (PC-1) → restored (4.3, fast glance) → held (4.5, long look) → echoed (4.6, brief check before accepting next order).
- **Knock three times** — same rhythm in 1.2 and 4.6. The line is identical: "他敲了三下。"

## The one thing this story must feel like

**A delivery that was completed on time, to the wrong address, by someone who stopped checking the name on the package.** The world is not hostile — it's just already on its phone.
