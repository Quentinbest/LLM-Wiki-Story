# Crisis-Climax Audit — 魔尊账号 / Wrong Account

Mode: POST-DRAFT (final-act prose 4.1–4.6 read against spine promises; plants checked at 2.4 and 3.7).

## 1. The Crisis （危机 / Crisis）

> **What the protagonist is forced to choose between** (staged 4.1–4.2, stairwell between 8F/9F, 02:14, smoke in the well):
> - **Option A** — pick up the *left* phone (苏寒's live call still connected) and walk out as 魔尊·墨渊: accept the only being-seen ever offered him.
> - **Option B** — refuse both names; speak his legal identity into the building intercom: recover the administrative truth and accept that the people who came for him will leave and forget his face.

| Cost of A | Cost of B |
|---|---|
| Ratifies that 陈默 was never real; he survives but as a borrowed name; the one private dignity he never spoke aloud (角色文件: "用别人的名字换回来的存在感，他用不起") is forfeit. | Recovers the legal name but the recovery is *administrative*; the seven faces that knew his face leave within days; he returns to the invisibility that was the original wound. |

- **Is each cost irreplaceable?** Yes. A costs the only dignity he has; B costs the only being-seen he has ever felt. Neither is recoverable by the other path. The Character File frames both as 真实的好 (genuine goods), not as a temptation-vs-right-answer.
- **Antagonism that closes off every other option:** The system has revoked his Meituan credential (PC-1, 1.5), kin failed to recognize him (PC-2), the kind savior (林晟) erases him even while apologizing (PC-6), and the crossfire wound to 李奶奶 (PC-7 / 3.7) makes staying-between-names cost a stranger blood. Every off-ramp is closed before 4.1. Not writer fiat.
- **Whose dimension does this fork test?** Chen Mo's terminal contradiction (Dimension Chart row 4): morally the simplest man carries the story's hardest dilemma; only he can choose. Also tests "想被看见 vs. 把生活组织成隐形."
- **Verdict:** **TRUE DILEMMA.** See removal test in §6 point 2.

## 2. The Climax （高潮 / Climax）

- **The Climactic action** (4.3): he presses the intercom and says, flat, in the exact cadence he has used to read 几千个 strangers' addresses — *"我是陈默。外卖员。工号 887423。北区站点。这个楼里没有魔尊。"*
- **Caused by:** the Crisis decision in 4.2 — the withheld reflex (hand reaches for the credential pocket, *withdraws*, both hands go to the wall, he steps over the two phones without touching them). The action flows directly from the choice; no third party acts for him.
- **Major Dramatic Question:** *Will Chen Mo prove he is not 魔尊·墨渊 — at the cost of returning to invisibility?*
- **Answer delivered:** **YES, ironically.** The 魔尊 overlay (the grey fog-mark on his wrist) peels and falls to his shoe (4.3); the app reads 陈默/评级0（恢复中）; 林晟 says the real name for the first time and apologizes (4.4). The audience receives the *yes* at the wrist-mark peeling; the *hollow* undertow at the receipt-tone ("一种他过去八天里一次都没听见过的、正常的、工作的声音") and at 林晟's footsteps leaving in Chen Mo's own dispatch cadence (4.4).
- **Genre obligatory scene fulfilled:** the highest-priority obligatory scene per genre-contract §1.2 #1 (face-to-face-with-the-strongest-antagonist-as-Crisis) is delivered at 4.1–4.3, where the "antagonist" is correctly rendered as the two-phone dilemma structure, not a person. The ironic-hollow-victory obligatory scene (§1.2 #3, 不可协商) is delivered at 4.6. PASS.
- **Inevitable in retrospect?** Yes. The administrative-declaration form is set up by the frozen-credential motif (1.5), the work-ID asked aloud at 2.4, and the building's emergency systems left active by the 3.7 fight. The receipt-tone callback ("和那夜对讲机里那一声回执音，是同一种声音") in 4.6 retroactively seals it.
- **Unexpected in form?** Yes — the genre primes a duel/awakening (the xianxia skin). The Climax is a man speaking a work ID into a broadcast panel he must stand on tiptoe to reach. The reader does not predict the *form* (declarative bureaucratic speech) though they feel its inevitability after.
- **Final value flip:** misnamed-but-seen → correctly-named (external **+**); simultaneously seen → invisible (internal **−**). Magnitude vs. earlier flips: largest — it is the only flip that resolves all three conflict levels at once and inverts external and internal charge in opposite directions. PASS.

## 3. Controlling Idea on the page

> **Controlling Idea**: 身份被夺回却空洞，因为无名才是真正的伤口，而非错名。 (Identity reclaimed but hollow; namelessness, not misidentification, was the real wound.)

| Element | Where it is dramatized (not stated) |
|---|---|
| 身份被夺回 (reclaimed) | 4.3 — overlay peels, app reads 陈默; 4.4 — 林晟 speaks the true name. |
| 却空洞 (but hollow) | 4.6 — 603 woman never looks at his face, "放门口就行," door's metal click is the *same sound* as the Climax receipt-tone "只是这一次，它没有纠正任何东西." |
| 无名才是真正的伤口 (namelessness was the real wound) | 4.5 — the name field 陈默 "从头到尾……一直是对的" (the misnaming was never the wound; the field was always correct and still nobody waited); 4.6 — lips part, no name spoken. |

**No character states the Idea.** The closest articulation (苏寒's 3.3 "你做的事，世界终于看见了。只是它叫你魔尊。") is diagnostic of the *temptation*, not the Idea's resolution, and lands before the Climax. The final value flip carries the Idea entirely through image and a withheld action. PASS — this is exemplary dramatize-don't-explain.

## 7. Coincidence / Deus-Ex-Machina Check (the load-bearing question)

The Climax mechanism — *the fused system accepts a declarative self-statement and corrects the identity* — is the single point where a deus ex machina could hide. Verdict: **properly planted, not arbitrary.**

- **Plant 1 (rule established), Scene 2.4:** when 林晟 repeats Chen Mo's own words back ("你说的是——'我从小就晕针'——对吧?"), the tea surface ripples (杯里的茶面起了一道纹). The world physically reacts to *declarative speech about identity being registered/repeated.* 老马 half-notices; 林晟 (head down) does not; Chen Mo sees it and straightens. The rule is shown, not told, ~22% into the book.
- **Plant 2 (the channel exists & is live), Scene 3.7:** the building's emergency systems are active because of the fight — the broken intercom's red LED is shown blinking at the scene's close (墙上那台坏了的对讲机，红色 LED 在闪). The intercom is established as present in the diegesis before 4.1.
- **Plant 3 (the form is his), Scene 2.4 + Character File:** the work-ID 887423 is asked aloud and answered at 2.4; the address-reading cadence is his three-year occupational reflex. At Climax he reports *himself* in that cadence. The form of the Climax is the protagonist's own established behavior, not a new power.
- **Agency at the Climax:** Chen Mo presses the button (4.3 "他用右手按下通话键"). No faction lowers weapons until *after* he speaks; 林晟 arrives *after* the overlay has already peeled (4.4). The system does not self-correct; it responds to his act.

**One residual note, not a flag:** the 2.4 plant fires on 林晟 *repeating Chen Mo's words*, whereas the 4.3 Climax fires on Chen Mo *speaking his own words into a system channel*. The rule a reader infers from 2.4 is loosely "the world registers spoken identity-truth"; 4.3 sharpens it to "declared into a system node, it overrides." This is a permissible *tightening* of an established rule, not a new rule — the teacup ripple already established that spoken identity-assertion has physical weight in this world. It would be marginally stronger if one mid-book beat had shown declarative speech producing a *system* (not just physical) response, but the frozen-app motif (1.5) plus the live intercom (3.7) cover the system channel sufficiently. **No deus ex machina.**
