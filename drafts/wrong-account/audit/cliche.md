# Cliché Hunt — 《魔尊账号》

Fresh read of all 26 prose scenes against the genre contract (幻灭剧 > 身份讽刺 > 黑色喜剧 > 仙侠壳) and the 裴鸣 persona bar (no narrated inner monologue, no emotion labels, no decorative darkness, no villain-cliché antagonists).

The prose is, overall, remarkably disciplined. The bulk of "stock-looking" material is genuinely earned through specificity (the App name-bar, the sensor-light rhythm, "放门口就行"). The true clichés are few and concentrated in two places: (a) a small handful of narrator-interprets-for-you lines that violate the persona's forbidden "他知道/她不是在问…是在问" register; (b) two anti-genre risks where the prose tells us what something means rather than letting the object carry it.

---

## 1. Protected conventions / motifs (DO NOT FLAG)

These look like stock moves but are required by the contract or are declared intentional motifs. Confirmed clean.

- **App name-bar 陈默/墨渊 (1.5, 4.3, 4.5, 4.6)** — core identity-satire mechanism (contract §2.1 #1). The four appearances are deliberately differentiated (slow→fast→slowest→glance). Motif, not cliché.
- **Sensor-light 12-second on/off (4.1–4.4)** — declared intentional rhythm device; it is doing real structural work (the dark/light beats meter the Crisis). Motif.
- **"放门口就行" (4.6)** — the controlling-idea landing beat, dramatized not stated. Convention fulfilled in owned form.
- **Door-clang mirroring the intercom receipt tone (4.6 line 30)** — explicitly an echo system the project planted. Motif. (Borderline-explained — see §2.6 #1.)
- **Grey-blue → orange light progression (1.1, 3.3, 3.4)** — declared light motif. The orange in 3.3 is specific (rooftop concrete warming, rusted flagpole "像是热的"). Earned.
- **Cold/dim 力场 at 3.7 (灯灭→冷色→黑色覆盖→十秒消退)** — anti-genre power "display" delivered as symptom, not awakening (contract §6, §8). NOT a xianxia awakening cliché. Protected.
- **林晟's "一个问题，一个'好'" interrogation (1.4, 2.3–2.5)** — the identity-interrogation obligatory scene (contract §2.2 #1), delivered in specific, owned form (Moleskine, A+, 咸豆浆). Convention.
- **The rooftop meeting (3.3)** — deepest-temptation obligatory scene. The cigarette / red-beacon staging is specific and the antagonist refuses villain-cadence (see §2.7). Convention.
- **The intercom administrative climax (4.3)** — anti-convention (climax is a declaration, not a duel). Planted (1.2/1.3/3.6) and paid for with the 3.7 pseudo-duel. Protected.
- **李奶奶 "……谁啊？" (3.7)** — the suppressed-violence black-comedy beat. The lightest possible three words. Convention.
- **Eating the abandoned 麻辣香锅 (1.2)** — structural (not verbal) black comedy. Owned.

## 3. The narrated-realization problem (the persona's #1 forbidden move)

This is the **dominant pattern** and the report's most important finding. The persona forbids "他知道 / 他意识到 / 她不是在问…是在问" — narrator entering a head or interpreting a beat. The prose mostly obeys, but leaks in a cluster:

| # | Where | Line | Verdict | Remediation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| N1 | 2.7 title + line 64 | "他知道它在哪儿" (also the scene title) | **TRUE CLICHÉ / persona violation** | This is the exact forbidden "他知道" construction, and it's load-bearing (last line + title). **Remediate by externalizing**: he already physically registered the menu's position (rch柜→桌上). End on the object or the hand, not on "他知道." E.g. end on "手搁在口袋边沿" (line 60, already there) and cut line 64. Retitle the scene. |
| N2 | 3.1 line 18 | "他说不清为什么，但他知道这一条" | **persona violation (mild)** | "他知道" again. Cut "但他知道这一条"; the action (refusing to bring paper and phone into the same room) already shows it. |
| N3 | 3.6 line 36–37 | "陈默听懂了那句话底下的意思：外面不打，是因为里面更好办。" | **TRUE CLICHÉ / violation** | narrator decodes subtext for the reader. **Cut the colon-and-gloss**; "不会在外面打，进楼" already plants it for an attentive reader. The persona wants the duel-disguised-as-small-talk to stay disguised. |
| N4 | 3.8 line 37–39 | "他对她的整个生活了如指掌。她对他这个人一无所知。" | **borderline → trim** | aphoristic narrator-summary of the irony. Powerful but it *states the theme*. Consider cutting; the kept钥匙 + "你是楼里的？" already dramatize it. |
| N5 | 3.8 line 45 | "她不是在问'你是谁'。她是在问'我是不是该认识你'。" | **TRUE CLICHÉ / violation** | the "不是X…是Y" interpretive construction — narrator telling us what the question means. **Cut**; let "……你是楼里的？" stand alone. This is the clearest persona breach in the corpus. |
| N6 | 3.8 line 51–52 | "和早上那杯咸豆浆是同一个动作——他又一次选择不说自己是谁。" | **violation (cross-scene narrator)** | narrator ranks/links beats across scenes — explicitly forbidden ("无跨场景叙述者排名"). Cut the gloss. |
| N7 | 4.4 line 50 | "像一个人收了工，往回走，不必赶，也没什么可留恋" | **borderline** | interpretive simile attributing 林晟's interior. Softer than N1–N6 because framed as 陈默's read of footsteps; defensible. Keep, monitor. |

**Pattern: the narrated-realization leaks cluster in 3.8 (three instances) and the 2.7/3.1/3.6 stretch.** Act 1, Act 2 (except 2.7's ending), and all of Act 4 (except the 4.6 echo-gloss ST1) hold the line cleanly. Scene 3.8 is the single weakest scene for persona discipline — it repeatedly steps out to explain the李奶奶 irony the staging already delivers.

## 5. Five-Point Cliché Audit
- [x] Conventions protected — no false positives; all contract conventions left standing.
- [x] Clichés categorized by family.
- [x] Every cliché has a remediation route.
- [x] Inversions are paid for — one partial gap (I2 reflection at 3.1).

## 6. Prioritized fix list (highest impact first)

1. **N5 (3.8 "她不是在问…是在问")** — cut. Clearest persona breach; sits in the emotional center of the李奶奶 sequence.
2. **N1 (2.7 "他知道它在哪儿" + title)** — externalize and retitle. Load-bearing last line of a key act-2 beat.
3. **N3 (3.6 "陈默听懂了那句话底下的意思…")** — cut the gloss; preserve duel-as-small-talk disguise.
4. **N6 (3.8 cross-scene "和早上那杯咸豆浆是同一个动作")** — cut; forbidden narrator ranking.
5. **I2 (3.1 screen-reflection 半张脸)** — cut the half-face; the only image-cliché flinch.
6. **ST1 (4.6 echo-gloss "只是这一次，它没有纠正任何东西")** — cut explanatory clause; trust the identical sound.
7. **N2, N4, N7, T1, S1, I3** — secondary trims (single clauses / mood-rain / rose reprise / "不是问句").

## 7. Open questions for the writer
- 2.7's title is "他知道它在哪儿." Is the title meant to be ironic-internal, or should it externalize like other scene titles (objects/lines)?
- 3.8 leans on three narrator-glosses to land the李奶奶 irony. Was this a deliberate "let the reader have it" choice, or drift? My read: the staging (the kept钥匙, "你是楼里的？") already delivers it; the glosses are insurance the persona doesn't want.
- S1 rain (1.2): duration-fact or mood? "变大了一点" is the tell.
