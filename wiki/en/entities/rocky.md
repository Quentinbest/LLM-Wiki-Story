# Rocky (1976)

> 中文版：[[wiki/zh/entities/rocky|中文]]

## Overview
A small-time Philadelphia boxer gets an unlikely chance to fight the heavyweight champion and discovers what he can endure.

## McKee's Usage
A frequent example of a **protagonist with dual desire** — the conscious object (go the distance with Apollo) disguising a deeper unconscious one (prove self-worth). The [[spine]] is Rocky's pursuit of dignity; the [[obligatory-scene]] is the final-round bell, not the decision on the scorecards.

## Concepts Illustrated
- **[[inciting-incident]]** — The offer to fight Apollo Creed.
- **[[object-of-desire]]** — Conscious: go the distance. Unconscious: be somebody.
- **[[spine]]** — The quest for self-worth through endurance.
- **[[obligatory-scene]]** — Lasting the final round.
