# Jaws (1975)

> 中文版：[[wiki/zh/entities/jaws|中文]]

## Overview
A New England beach town sheriff hunts a great white shark terrorizing its waters. Adapted from Peter Benchley's novel.

## McKee's Usage
Cited for the textbook placement of the [[inciting-incident]] (the first attack), its [[progressive-complications]] across three acts closing on increasingly irrevocable [[points-of-no-return]], and the finally delivered [[obligatory-scene]] — Brody alone against the shark.

## Concepts Illustrated
- **[[inciting-incident]]** — Chrissie's death.
- **[[points-of-no-return]]** — Closing the beach; hiring Quint; the boat going out.
- **[[progressive-complications]]** — The shark keeps outclassing each new plan.
- **[[obligatory-scene]]** — The final showdown at sea.
