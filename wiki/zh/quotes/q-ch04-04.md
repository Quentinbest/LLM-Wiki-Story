# 引文 ch.4-4

> English: [[wiki/en/quotes/q-ch04-04|English]]

> "Do not write something because intellectual friends think it's socially important… Be honest in your choice of genre, for of all the reasons for wanting to write, the only one that nurtures us through time is love of the work itself."
> ——罗伯特·麦基《故事》第4章
